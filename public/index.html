"pyt med det"

"Pyt med det" is the perfect phrase for anyone who want to live a stress-free life. It's like saying "Don't worry, be happy," but with a Scandinavian twist. Think of it as the Danish equivalent of "Hakuna Matata" from The Lion King. So next time you're feeling frazzled, just take a deep breath, channel your inner Viking, and say "Pyt med det!" Who knows, it might just become your new life motto. X
